Secure on-line payments

   
 

The Teach Ghana Trust's objects tend to have costs associated with them; sending books and clothes to Ghana usually costs money and so your financial backing is very important to us. Further to this, we aim to assist schools in Ghana in any way which we feel is beneficial to the students of the school e.g. it is in our opinion that a school will benefit from having electricity at their site to operate fans in the high temperatures and to
offer some light when thunder storms occur so that lessons can continue as normal.

Not only does this help the students and teachers at the school but also assists the Electricity board(s) of Ghana which in turn will be able to spend more money on ensuring that the regular power failures which occur in Ghana become less frequent. To acomplish such a task they may need to recruit more which they will be able to afford using the money provided from the new custom. So any financial contributions to our charity will be gratefully received.
