Donate books and clothes
   
 

It is very easy (here in Britain) to take items such as books, clothes and stationery for granted. In Ghana items such as these (whether second hand or new) are treated as items of luxury. Before throwing items you no longer need away, consider donating them to a school in Ghana where one basic reading book could help several illiterate children learn to read or where an old t-shirt might be the first gift a child has received in his life.
