Grant Requests

   
 

If your School or College would like to request a grant from the Teach Ghana Trust, then you can either fill in and submit the form electronically (below), or download and print out a copy of the form here.

If you choose to submit electronically, please not that we will still require a signed and dated paper copy should your grant be approved.

Good luck with your application!

Terms and Conditions

Please read the following terms and conditions of a grant from the Teach Ghana Trust:

• Funds will only be released by the Trust on receipt of an invoice showing the costs of the items to be purchased / built.
• Receipts must be provided following expenditure.
• You will be required to produce a short report one year after the grant is made showing the items purchased / built and giving a brief summary of the institution’s activities over the last year with apanying photographs.
