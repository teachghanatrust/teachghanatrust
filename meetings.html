26.3.2006
   
 

Meeting of the Teach Ghana Trust trustees and organisers.

An important vote was taken by the trustees of the Teach Ghana Trust with regards to the stance that the charity should take on the arrangement of short-term volunteer placements in Ghana.

The trustees voted against the motion "Should the TGT arrange volunteer teaching placements in Ghana?" by 4 votes to 3 with no abstentions.
